Chess Plus Rules Reference

This file lists the current rules in plain text, along with their main traits.

Trait guide:
- Kind: permanent, temporary, or instant
- Rarity: common, uncommon, rare, or legendary
- Unique: if true, the rule cannot appear while it is already active
- Duration: only used for temporary rules

1. Backstep Pawns
- Kind: permanent
- Rarity: common
- Unique: true
- Effect: All pawns gain one-square backward moves and backward captures.

2. Knighted Kings
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: true
- Duration: 3 to 8 moves, even only
- Effect: Kings gain knight jumps in addition to their normal move.

3. Diagonal Rooks
- Kind: permanent
- Rarity: common
- Unique: true
- Effect: Rooks may also step one square diagonally, and those diagonal steps can capture.

4. Orthogonal Bishops
- Kind: permanent
- Rarity: common
- Unique: true
- Effect: Bishops may also step one square orthogonally, and those orthogonal steps can capture.

5. He Has a Bomb
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: common
- Unique: false
- Duration: 4 to 8 moves, even only
- Effect: Choose one of your non-king pieces. When the timer ends, that piece and all adjacent pieces explode.

6. Double Time
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: true
- Duration: 2 normal-turn triggers per side
- Effect: Both players get an extra move after each of their next 2 normal turns. Bonus turns do not chain into more bonus turns.

7. Board Expansion
- Kind: permanent
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: false
- Effect: Add one empty tile to every edge of the board, expanding it outward in all four directions. This can happen multiple times in one game. Pawn promotion always uses the farthest rank, so the new outer ranks become promotion zones.

8. Ceasefire
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: uncommon
- Unique: true
- Duration: 2 to 6 moves, even only
- Effect: Captures are forbidden.

9. Royal Sprint
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: true
- Duration: 4 to 8 moves, even only
- Effect: Kings may step up to two squares in any direction, but they cannot move through pieces.

10. Cavalry Doctrine
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: common
- Unique: true
- Duration: 3 to 7 moves, odd only
- Effect: The chooser's knights may also step diagonally one square.

11. Heavy Weather
- Kind: temporary
- Rarity: uncommon
- Unique: true
- Duration: 4 to 7 moves, even or odd
- Effect: Each move must either be a capture, a pawn move, or a king move.

12. Pawnstorm
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: common
- Unique: false
- Effect: Every pawn you own tries to advance one square immediately if the square is empty.

13. Reinforcements
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: false
- Effect: Place new pawns for both sides on every empty square of Black's 2nd rank and White's 7th rank.

14. Open the Graves
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: legendary
- Unique: false
- Effect: All currently captured pieces return on random empty squares in the first two rows on their own side. Recalled or otherwise revived pieces do not count unless they were captured again later.

15. Recall
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: rare
- Unique: false
- Effect: Return your most recently captured non-king piece to the nearest open starting square.

16. Queen's Gift
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: legendary
- Unique: false
- Effect: Each player promotes their most advanced pawn into a queen where it stands, if they have one.

17. Royal Relocation
- Kind: instant
- Rarity: uncommon
- Unique: false
- Effect: Swap your king with your nearest rook.

Notes
- Draft timing is separate from individual rule traits.
- Temporary rules with a move range roll their exact duration when they appear in the draft choices, so players see the actual duration before picking.
- Unique rules are filtered out of the draft while active.
